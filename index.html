Forbes Billionaires of 2021
Forbes Magazine, well-known for its annual lists and rankings revealed that in 2021, there were a total of 2755 Billionaires and a wealth surge occured as the number escalated by 660.

Jeff Bezos topped the list for the fourth year in a row while Elon Musk soared to the second spot as both Amazon and Tesla shares skyrocketed in 2021.


United States of America has the greatest number of billionaires with 724 while China came second with 626 billionaires. India, Germany and Russia were next with each having more than 100 billionaires.



Amid the 2755 billionaires, majority of them aged between 51 to 70 while one-third of the billionaires aged between 71 to 100. Asia has the most billionaires with 1148 which is around 42%.
The industry with the highest total net worth is the Technology industry with a whopping amount of 2456.7 billion and the total number of billionaires is 365.



In North America, most billionaires ventured into the Finance & Investments and Technology industries. In Europe, the top venture is the Fashion & Retail industry whereas in Asia, the Manufacturing industry topped the list.
